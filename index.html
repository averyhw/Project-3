Composite Scores Meeting the UNC Requirements

The composite ACT scores are made up of the average scores of the reading, writing, science, math and english sections of the standardized test. Most schools accept the highest composite score, but some, like UNC, will accept a student's superscore, or composite of all the composites, guarunteeing the highest outcome for the applicant.
